
HCP LINKAGE MEMO — DRAFT

This memo quantifies the relationship between Botswana food inflation and regional food inflation indicators from the HCP cross-country panel. South Africa and Namibia are prioritised because of Botswana's regional trade and customs linkages, while Kenya and Zimbabwe are included as broader regional comparators.

Statistical evidence:
The notebook exports an OLS regression table with coefficients and p-values, plus Granger-causality p-values for regional food inflation indicators. This provides quantitative evidence rather than qualitative claims only.

Forward projection:
The team's 2024 food inflation forecast is converted into a base-scenario food-pressure proxy. Months above a 5% food inflation threshold are accumulated into a cumulative food pressure index, which can be interpreted as a simple household affordability stress signal relevant to human-capital outcomes such as nutrition, attendance, and household welfare pressure.
